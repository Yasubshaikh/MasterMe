MasterMe Free Quiz App

MasterMe is a free downloadable quiz and school web app for GK, current affairs, math, science, love GK, fun brain challenges, class notes, and video lessons. Players can answer questions, level up, earn coins, unlock achievements, share notes, and compete on a live-style leaderboard.

The app can be installed from a mobile browser as a free home-screen app. Admin-only editing lets the owner add new questions, change answers, manage school notes and videos, and export app content.
