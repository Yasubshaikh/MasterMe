Privacy Policy

MasterMe stores quiz progress, coins, shares, notes progress, and admin session state in the browser on the user's device. The current free prototype does not sell personal data.

If Google AdSense, Firebase, or another service is connected later, those services may process data according to their own privacy policies. The app owner should update this page before enabling ads, SMS login, analytics, or cloud accounts.

Students can clear local app data from their browser settings at any time.
